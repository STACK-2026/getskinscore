# Cookie Policy

*Last updated: April 13, 2026*

## What cookies do we use?

SkinScore uses minimal cookies:

### Essential cookies
- **Cookie consent preference** (`skinscore_cookie_consent`): stores your accept/decline choice. Local storage, never sent to servers.

### Analytics
- **Session tracking** (`sks_session_id`): anonymous session identifier stored in sessionStorage (cleared when you close the browser). Used to understand how people navigate the site. No personal data.
- **UTM tracking** (`sks_utm`): stores campaign parameters if you arrived via a marketing link. SessionStorage only.

## What we DON'T use

- No Google Analytics
- No Facebook Pixel
- No advertising cookies
- No cross-site tracking
- No third-party cookies
- No personal identification

## Your choices

- **Accept**: we collect anonymous page view data to improve the site
- **Decline**: we respect your choice, no analytics data collected
- You can clear all data by clearing your browser's local storage

## Data storage

Analytics data is stored on Supabase (EU region). It contains: page URL visited, device type, country (via Cloudflare), timestamp. No names, emails, or personal identifiers.
