# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: April 12, 2026*

## What data we collect

SkinScore collects minimal data to improve your experience:

- **Analytics**: page views, session duration, device type, country (via Cloudflare). No personal identification.
- **Cookies**: a single cookie consent preference stored in your browser. No tracking cookies.
- **Account data** (if you create one): email address, skin type preference, saved products.

## What we don't do

- We **never** sell your data to third parties
- We **never** share personal data with advertisers
- We **never** use invasive tracking (no Facebook Pixel, no Google Analytics)
- We **never** store your data longer than necessary

## Data storage

Data is stored on Supabase (EU region, GDPR compliant). Analytics are anonymised and aggregated.

## Your rights (GDPR)

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. Contact us at contact@getskinscore.com.

## Contact

For any privacy-related questions: **contact@getskinscore.com**
